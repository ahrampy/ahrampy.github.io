About Me

I have always had a passion for creation. I am interested in people and what they do, and how to share these experiences with others. I believe the more lives we come into contact with, the more joy we can find in our own. As a software developer, I strive to create opportunities to share experiences, to learn, and to build new pathways towards personal and social growth. I love seeing something I have built be enjoyed, or offer a new insight. I am always learning how to make better experiences through code, and hope to never stop improving.
